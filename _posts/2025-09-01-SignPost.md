SignPost: a materialized action interface for agentic graph RAG serving. It pre-computes, offline and deterministically, a per-object "sketch" of the agent's four retrieval actions (zoom / read / jump / verify) plus source locators, moving classic graph RAG's per-hop graph-neighborhood rebuilding off the online query path (inspired by database materialized views). With a budgeted controller using a fixed 2 LLM calls end-to-end, it attains the best macro-average answer quality across four domains (7.97/10) against 8 baselines (incl. RL-trained HiPRAG / GraphRAG-R1), with a large lead on MuSiQue evidence reachability (SilverHit@5 = 0.90). *(Manuscript under submission to ICDE 2027 — not yet peer-reviewed / accepted.)*
